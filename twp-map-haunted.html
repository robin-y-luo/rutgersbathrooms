This visual essay documents where in the world I acquired some of the items I've thrown away, recycled, or donated since starting this project in January 2019. The catalog below documents how, where, and when each item was acquired, why it was acquired, and how it was disposed off.

If you find this essay interesting, I recommend taking a look at its companion essay, Great Trash Cans from around the World, which documents some of the finer receptacles I've encountered on my travels.
