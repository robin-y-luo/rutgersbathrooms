Our Reviews at a Glance

Feel free to peruse our great assortment of bathroom reviews. Below, you will find a series of photos documenting various buildings around the university, primarily Livingston. We have taken the task of sorting them into various categories, for ease of discernment for our wonderful readers. In “Exceptional Bathrooms Spotlight”, you’ll find our top recommendations, where you can truly place your trust in the sanctity of the porcelain bowl. In “Bathrooms That Were Acceptable”, you’ll find your average, run-of-the-mill bathrooms for your typical Joe or Josephine. Real salt of the earth bathrooms. In “Bathrooms That Were Absolutely Diabolical”, you’ll perhaps find bathrooms that made us reconsider our majors to drop everything and study like, bathroom engineering, or whatever, to secure a future with better bathrooms for our posterity. Thank you for trusting Rutgers Bathrooms, a subsidiary of the Rutgers Review, as your primary source for all news Rutgers bathrooms.
